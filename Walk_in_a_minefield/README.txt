Overview: Walk_in_a_minefield

Walk in a minefield is a challange posted on
a website.

The challange is to read a minefield from a file("mine.txt"), a series of commands("commands.txt")
,execute them and show if the path was succesful or not and
also show the path that the robot took.


The files "mine.txt" and "commands.txt" must be created in order to work!

More details about the challange at:

https://www.reddit.com/r/dailyprogrammer/comments/7d4yoe/20171114_challenge_340_intermediate_walk_in_a/
